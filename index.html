THE INCEPTION

The eagle has symbolized unmatched freedom, raw power, and soaring grace for millennia—from the Roman Aquila, sacred to Jupiter and carried as the standard of conquering legions, to the American bald eagle, the enduring emblem of resilient leadership and unyielding sovereignty.

This spirit lives on in citizen-soldiers like Cincinnatus, who left his farm to defend Rome and then voluntarily surrendered power—an inspiration to George Washington, who led a revolution to victory before returning to civilian life at Mount Vernon. That same ethos of service, sacrifice, and restraint for the Republic forges Sonic Eagles: proudly American, built in honor of every service member who has bled, sweated, and fought to defend this nation.

Founder and Executive Producer Rome Coffey also draws inspiration from his father’s eight years of service in the U.S. Army Reserve and cherished memories at Fort Hamilton, Brooklyn as a young boy. He launches the label with his debut single, "Eagle Flies," a bold celebration of Liberation Day and the reclamation of America’s founding promise.

The name Sonic Eagles fuses the legendary "Screaming Eagles" of the 101st Airborne Division—where guitar icon Jimi Hendrix served as a paratrooper, earned his wings, and received the Screaming Eagles patch—with Hendrix’s revolutionary sound and visionary spirit. Sonic Eagles isn’t just music; it’s a sonic manifesto for America’s emerging golden era of abundance.

An independent record label rooted in America First principles spanning many genres, it champions the sovereignty of We The People through hard-hitting riffs, patriotic anthems, and unfiltered truth.

At the heart of the upcoming Sonic Eagles Rockumentary which will be announced soon stand two powerful compositions that chart the rebirth of American strength through President Trump’s cabinet: "PAX Americana Pt. 1," a soaring psychedelic anthem igniting the vision of a renewed golden age of peace through unapologetic strength, and "Stand Sovereign (PAX Americana Pt. 2)," a thunderous hard-rock declaration calling We The People to rise, secure our borders, reject forever wars, reclaim sovereignty with fire in our veins and to fight, fight, fight! Together, these tracks form a bold sonic manifesto blending raw riffs, patriotic fury, and unfiltered truth, capturing the spirit of America First as it surges into a new era of abundance, resilience, and unbreakable will.
